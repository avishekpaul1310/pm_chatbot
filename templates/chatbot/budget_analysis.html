Budget Utilization Tips
Tips for Under Budget Projects
  • Consider allocating additional resources if the project is behind schedule.
  • Re-evaluate the budget to ensure all necessary costs have been accounted for.
  • Document successful cost-saving strategies for future projects.
  • Consider if quality is being sacrificed for cost savings.
Tips for Over Budget Projects
  • Identify the root causes of budget overruns.
  • Implement cost control measures immediately.
  • Re-prioritize project tasks to focus on essential deliverables.
  • Consider requesting additional funding if the project scope has expanded.
